The combined effect of May's 16-SKU sub-category catalog launch and the dedicated K-Beauty LATAM 1-pager is now the leading inbound source in ERGSN's pipeline. Procurement teams browsing the catalog by routine step (Toner / Ampoule / Cream / Primer / Cushion / Setting Mist) consolidate into multi-SKU private-label package deals rather than single-SKU spot orders — the highest-value pattern from a sourcing-platform economics standpoint.

Two consolidation patterns dominate the early data:

  • MENA premium retail — ampoule-heavy (2–3 SKUs) + barrier cream. Dermaceutical positioning lands cleanly against the Halal-aware regulatory environment.

K-Tourism Assets opens the second-partner intake via the new partners-tourism.html landing. The recruitment page documents the four-step verification track (Discovery → On-site Audit → Documentation Review → Long-term Partnership) that brought Yangpyeong Soohyang live in May.

Eligibility is intentionally narrow this quarter: traditional hanok or hanok-style stand-alone properties, 5+ rooms or campus operation, licensed lodging registration, fire / liability insurance, and family-run or small-hospitality operator profile. Large hotel chains are out of scope for this partnership shape.

Korean domestic OTAs cover nightly room sales already; ERGSN K-Tourism's value is the channels Korean operators do not reach in their own language — foreign corporate retreat planners, global hospitality portfolio operators, and gujwa-bunyang fractional-share investors who require English-language onboarding.
