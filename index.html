Who We Are

Korean Precision.
American Standards.

ERGSN CO., LTD. is a Seoul-based manufacturer and exporter of heavy-duty industrial paper shredders. We design and produce the DL Series cross-cut shredders to meet the rigorous demands of U.S. commercial, healthcare, legal, and government procurement.

Every ERGSN shredder uses an all-metal chain drive — eliminating the nylon and plastic gears found in lower-quality alternatives. Machines built to operate reliably for years, not months.

Long-term supply partner of Capital Shredder Corp. (Rockville, MD), delivering CIF Baltimore on schedule across multiple purchase orders.
