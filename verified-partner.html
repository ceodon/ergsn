Usage guidelines

The badge is issued under a revocable licence. Partners may display it on their own websites, product brochures, email signatures, and trade-show collateral — provided the following rules are observed:

Do

Link the badge back to ceodon.github.io/ergsn. Keep the original colours (navy shield + gold seal). Scale proportionally. Display alongside your own branding — the badge is a credential, not a replacement.

Do not

Do not alter the shield shape, swap colours, add words, crop the text, or use it on unrelated products. Do not imply ERGSN ownership of your company. Misuse terminates the licence immediately.

When verification lapses

Verification is renewed yearly. If a partner fails a renewal audit, they have 30 days to remove the badge from all materials. ERGSN updates the public partner list accordingly.

Challenging misuse

Buyers who suspect an unverified company is displaying the badge can report it — ERGSN maintains the authoritative partner list and will issue a written confirmation on request.

Apply to become a Verified Partner

The Verified Partner credential is available to Korean manufacturers that complete the 4-step audit. Start by submitting the partnership enquiry form — ERGSN will reply within one business day.
