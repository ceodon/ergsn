Frequently Asked

Common questions about the free tools.

Are ERGSN's Korea trade tools really free?

Yes. Landed Cost, HS Lookup, Compliance Checker, and Document Templates require no signup and have no usage caps for individual buyers. ERGSN's revenue comes from completed shipments, not gated tools.

Which Korean ports does the Landed Cost Calculator cover?

14 ports — Busan, Incheon, Pyeongtaek, Gwangyang, Ulsan, Gunsan, Yeosu, Donghae, Pohang, Masan, Mokpo, Jeju, Incheon Airport (ICN), and Gimhae Airport (PUS-air).

Does the HS code lookup support Korea-specific tariff schedules?

Yes. Export Documents returns HS6 codes plus the Korean export tariff under applicable FTAs (KORUS, KAFTA, RCEP, EU, UK, ASEAN, India) and required certifications (KFDA, KS, KCC, etc.).

Are the Commercial Invoice and Packing List templates valid for customs?

Yes. Templates follow standard international trade format (Incoterms 2020, ISO 4217 currency codes) and are used in production by Korean exporters working with ERGSN. Always have your destination broker review before first shipment.

Is Korea sanction-safe for global trade?

Korea is a TAA-compliant origin and not subject to US, EU, or UN sectoral sanctions. ERGSN's verified network is pre-screened against OFAC SDN. The forthcoming Sanction-Safe Verification tool will let buyers screen any Korean manufacturer in seconds.
