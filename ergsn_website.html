Who We Are

Korean Precision.
American Standards.

ERGSN CO., LTD. is a Seoul-based manufacturer and exporter of heavy-duty industrial paper shredders. Headquartered in Joong-gu, Seoul, we design and produce the DL Series cross-cut shredders to meet the rigorous demands of U.S. commercial, healthcare, legal, and government procurement.

Every ERGSN shredder is built with all-metal chain drive mechanisms — eliminating nylon and plastic gears found in lower-quality alternatives. Our engineering philosophy prioritizes durability over disposability: machines that operate reliably for years, not months.

We have maintained a long-term supply relationship with Capital Shredder Corp. (Rockville, MD), delivering CIF Baltimore on schedule across multiple purchase orders. Our export documentation, compliance certifications, and T/T payment terms are designed for frictionless U.S. import operations.
